## About Me

I am a first-year Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning student at Bursa Technical University.  
I aim to strengthen the theoretical knowledge I gain both at university and through various online platforms by applying it to practical projects.

I am also a member of the software team of the **Poyraz Team**, operating under the Aerospace and Defense Industry Society at our university.  
Within the team, I focus on model training and computer vision.

In addition to technical skills, this experience also helps me improve important professional competencies such as teamwork, problem-solving, and stress management.
