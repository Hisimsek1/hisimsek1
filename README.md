## About

I'm a first-year **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning** student at **Bursa Technical University**.

I focus on turning theoretical knowledge into real projects — whether that's building predictive models, exploring datasets, or working on computer vision systems.

I'm also part of **Poyraz Team**, the software team under our university's Aerospace & Defense Industry Society, where I work on **model training and computer vision** for UAV/İHA systems.

Beyond the technical side, the team has sharpened skills I value just as much — problem-solving under pressure, collaboration, and staying calm when things break.
